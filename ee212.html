Course Info.

Course descriptions

  • Elementary making class using Arduinos

    • Interfacing with Arduino (sensors, actuators, and elementary circuits)

    • Arduino programming (C-language)

  • Team projects (of 2-4 persons)

    • Engineering systems design and prototyping

Instructors
